About This Service

A TV that is slightly crooked, mounted into drywall with no stud, or surrounded by dangling cables is one of those things that bothers you every time you look at it. Mount N Muscle makes sure you never have that problem.

We mount TVs of all sizes on all surface types throughout the Charlotte metro — drywall, brick, tile, stone, concrete, and above fireplaces. Every mount is stud-located and properly anchored so it is secure for the long term.

Cable management is included on every TV mounting job. Cables run cleanly behind the wall or through a cable raceway so the finished result looks intentional. The difference between a professional mount and a DIY mount is immediately visible.

We bring all the tools required for a professional install — stud finders, levels, drill bits for various wall types, cable raceways, and anchors appropriate for each surface. You do not need to have anything on hand other than the TV and the mount.

TV mounting pricing in Charlotte starts at $75 for a standard single-screen install. Gallery walls, multiple TVs, above-fireplace mounts, and complex cable runs are quoted on-site.

We mount TVs throughout Charlotte, Huntersville, Concord, Mooresville, Matthews, Pineville, Ballantyne, Mount Holly, Gastonia, Waxhaw, Stallings, Indian Trail, Fort Mill, Tega Cay, Lake Wylie, and Rock Hill.
