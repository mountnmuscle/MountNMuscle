About This Service

Moving is already stressful enough without having to worry about whether the crew will show up, handle your belongings carefully, or hit you with surprise fees at the end. Mount N Muscle keeps it simple — we show up, we handle it right, and we charge exactly what we quoted.

We provide local moving services throughout the Charlotte metro area for apartments, houses, condos, and offices. Whether you are moving across town in Ballantyne or relocating from Concord to Fort Mill, we bring the muscle and the truck to make it happen efficiently.

Every move is handled personally — no subcontractors, no strangers. You are dealing with the same operator from the first call to the final piece of furniture in place. That consistency makes a real difference when you are trusting someone with everything you own.

We offer full truck-assisted moving as well as loading and unloading help if you have rented your own vehicle. Need help moving heavy furniture between rooms or up a flight of stairs? We handle that too — no job is too small or too specific.

Moving pricing is based on the size of the load and distance involved. We assess on-site before quoting so the number is always accurate and fair. No hourly surprises, no fuel surcharges added at the end — what we quote is what you pay.

We serve the full Charlotte metro for moving including Charlotte, Huntersville, Concord, Mooresville, Matthews, Pineville, Ballantyne, Mount Holly, Gastonia, Waxhaw, Stallings, Indian Trail, Fort Mill, Tega Cay, Lake Wylie, and Rock Hill.
